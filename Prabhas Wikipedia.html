Prabhas

This article is about the actor born in 1979. For other uses, see Prabhas (disambiguation).

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju( born 23 October 1979), known mononymously as Prabhas , is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Telugu cinema.[4] He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015.[9] Referred to in the media as Rebel Star, he has appeared in over 20 films, and has received seven Filmfare Awards nominations, a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award
